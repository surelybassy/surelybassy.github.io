Beccy Ashdown

She / Her

Creative Producer - Impact Strategist - Facilitator - Connector

Hi! I’m Beccy,

Thanks for visiting!

My work explores the intersections between social change, creativity and the arts. I’m driven by collaboration, community, and creative exploration. I love connecting people and organisations to create unique and memorable experiences, but that online or in the real world.

I enjoy working across artforms, and have particular experience in documentary film and participatory theatre. I’ve had the privilege of working in a range of settings from prisons to schools and community groups, public spaces and cultural venues.

I am currently Campaign Director for Together Films, overseeing creative impactful distribution campaigns. Prior to this I worked for the British Film Institute as Events and Production Manager, as well as working on the BFI London Film Festival for many years.

I’ve worked with a range of organisations including TIFF, Amnesty International UK, BBC Media Action & the Royal Society of Arts.

I’m a Birds Eye View Future Leaders in Distribution alumni, and a member of the Impact Producers Group.

I grew up in Leeds & then studied Social & Cultural Studies at the University Nottingham and University of Toronto. I later went on to do an MA in Applied Theatre at Royal Central School of Speech & Drama. I’m currently undertaking my Yoga Teacher Training Diploma with YogaCampus, and am excited to incorporate these new skills into my practice. I live in London.

Interested in collaborating? Get in touch!
